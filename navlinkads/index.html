NavLink Ads!


NavLink Ads is an ad system for personal websites. It cycles between ads every 30 seconds. It does not host any commercial ADs, and it does not cost money to join. Every ad in the system is for personal websites only, and preferrably hand coded sites (so nothing made with a website builder).

Want your AD in the system? You can contact me on Discord, e-mail or any other social. Send me a link to the image as well as your site URL. If you don't want your bandwidth being used up, host it somewhere like Imgur. Do not use Catbox or Discord, they're not reliable. You MUST provide permament link to the image. I will not upload it for you.

You have to add the widget on your site before applying to promote other sites in the network.

ADS MUST BE 180x180 PIXELS.
Images shouldn't include words like "ad" "promo" "advert" etc in their titles. Most adblock systems flag it and it'll cause the widget to disappear. That includes directories as well.

I also suggest you don't put text too low on the image because it might get covered by the "NavLink Ads" box.

Oh, and one last thing, site should have some content. Get some cool things, writings, projects, arts you've made or whatever, don't submit your website if it's just info about you and blog with 1 post saying "hi i made a website", thanks.
